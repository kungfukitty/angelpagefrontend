Angel Kellogg Announces Johannesburg Expansion and Multi-Brand Ecosystem Growth

Angel Kellogg formalizes Johannesburg as a primary base, expanding content, cultural activations, and nonprofit services across ATL ↔ Jozi.

JOHANNESBURG, South Africa — September 30, 2025 — Entrepreneur and cultural architect Angel Kellogg announced Johannesburg as a primary base for content, community programs, and brand collaborations spanning the ATL ↔ Jozi corridor. The expansion consolidates work across five brands, aligning production, partnerships, and nonprofit services in one operating model.

Expansion Highlights

  • From the Block to the Blockchain: scaled Web3 education formats and UGC packs for partners.
  • ForeverDOCs: enhanced access to free family document preservation for South African communities.
  • BRGRCollective: skills pathways for women entering tech and Web3.
  • Jozi Experience: curated cultural immersion and innovation tours in Johannesburg.
  • YOH Underground: members-only nightlife experiences with crypto-only payments.

Quotes

“Jozi is where culture and ambition meet on the street every day. We’re building systems that turn that energy into ownership,” said Angel Kellogg.

About Angel Kellogg

Angel Kellogg builds attention-first brands that convert cultural energy into measurable outcomes. Work spans education, nonprofit infrastructure, and curated experiences across the US and South Africa.
